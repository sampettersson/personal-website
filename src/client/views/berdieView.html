Berdie

The adventures of a bird.

COLLECT COINS!

Use your phone to control Berdie into colliding with a coin to collect it.

Berdie is the frustrating game where you control a bird into colliding with coins to collect them, this with insanely annoying accelerometer based controls and a time limit.

Berdie was created as a part of a school assignment, where I was in charge of the code and art. Berdie was written in Swift and utilizes SpriteKit.
